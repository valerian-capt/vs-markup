Real 3d model viewer for iPhone & iPod touch

Viewshape is a novel 3D model viewing applicationdesigned for iPhone & iPod touch.

It lets you view 3D models of seal scanned objects or 3D models created by artists in modeling software. You can save models you like in the gallery and share models with your friends, customers and colleagues. You may download models directly from the Web, upload them with iTunes or open them from other applications.

A wide range of 3D file formats are supported.

Key Features

  • Easy and intuitive view navigation
  • OBJ, STL, VRML and PLY formats support
  • Supports for textures
  • Many different models of rendering including solid, wireframe and points
  • Download models form the web or open them from other applications (i.e. Mail.app)
  • Built-in gyroscope and accelerometer support
  • Full screen mode
  • Share links to models with friends
